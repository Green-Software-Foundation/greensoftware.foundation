green software foundation

The Green Software Foundation is a non-profit with the mission to create a trusted ecosystem of people, standards, tooling and best practices for building green software. Our ambition is a 50% reduction in the carbon emissions of software, via abatement, not neutralisation, by 2030.

Our Focus Areas

Standards: Agreeing on standards, best practices and patterns for building green software.
Innovation: Nurture the creation of trusted open-source and open-data projects and support academic research.
Community: Facilitate the growth of a diverse and international community of green software ambassadors.
