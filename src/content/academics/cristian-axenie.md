He holds [50+ peer-reviewed publications and 10+ patents](https://cristianaxenie.info/publications/), and brings 15+ years of academic research combined with 10+ years of industrial research — including six years as Staff Research Engineer at Huawei's largest R&D centre outside China, and as Principal Investigator at the Audi Konfuzius-Institut Ingolstadt Laboratory."
